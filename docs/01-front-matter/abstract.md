> The Community Affairs Office (CAO) of the Provincial Government of Camarines Norte administers the Camarines Norte Expanded Provincial Government High School Educational Assistance Program (CNEPGHSEAP), established under Provincial Ordinance No. 604-2022, which provides financial assistance to Grade 7 students across public and private schools in the province. In the school year 2023-2024, the program supported 12,024 students. However, the existing system faces significant operational challenges, including manual data encoding of beneficiary information, redundant data entry processes, and limited analytics capabilities for tracking program distribution across municipalities and schools. These inefficiencies create administrative bottlenecks, delay service delivery, and prevent data-driven decision-making.

> This project, conducted as part of Software Engineering 2 (SE102), analyzes and enhances the existing CNEPGHSEAP management system through systematic evaluation, redesign, and technological improvement. Using Agile development methodology, the enhanced system addresses critical gaps by automating data extraction from DepEd documents, implementing centralized database management, eliminating redundant encoding processes, and providing advanced analytics and reporting capabilities. The web-based solution streamlines administrative workflows, improves transparency and accountability, and enables efficient tracking of all 12,024 beneficiaries.

> The enhanced CNEPGHSEAP Scholar Management System enables the Community Affairs Office to manage scholarship administration more efficiently, respond promptly to beneficiary needs, and uphold the provincial government's commitment to providing equitable educational opportunities for all Grade 7 students in Camarines Norte.
